Are you in high school? Are you a Democrat? Do you feel like getting involved and having your voice heard in the political discourse? The New York High School Democrats exist to help organize young people around a common desire for progress in our state and across the country, and the belief that no one is too young to do something about it.
It is the proud tradition of our Democratic Party to be inviting of others into the mainstream, to give voice to those who would otherwise not be heard. In that spirit, we invite you to join with us.
